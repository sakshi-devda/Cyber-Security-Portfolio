# Introduction to Cyber Security

## What is Cyber Security?

Cyber Security is the practice of protecting computers, networks, systems, and data from cyber attacks, unauthorized access, damage, or theft.

---

## Why is Cyber Security Important?

- Protects personal and business data
- Prevents cyber attacks
- Ensures privacy
- Maintains business continuity
- Protects financial information

---

## Goals of Cyber Security

- Confidentiality
- Integrity
- Availability

These three principles are known as the CIA Triad.

---

## Common Types of Cyber Threats

- Malware
- Phishing
- Ransomware
- Denial of Service (DoS)
- SQL Injection
- Password Attacks

---

## Skills Required

- Networking
- Linux
- Python
- Web Security
- Problem Solving
- Communication

---

## Real-World Example

When you use online banking, cybersecurity helps protect your login credentials and financial transactions from attackers.

---

## Summary

Cyber Security is the process of protecting digital systems, networks, and information from cyber threats while ensuring confidentiality, integrity, and availability.
