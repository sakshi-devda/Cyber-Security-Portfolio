## 2. Objective

The objective of this room is to understand the CIA Triad, the fundamental security model used to protect information systems, and learn how Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability contribute to effective cybersecurity.

---

## 3. Introduction

The CIA Triad is one of the most important concepts in cybersecurity. It consists of three core security principles:

- Confidentiality
- Integrity
- Availability

These principles help organizations design secure systems, protect sensitive information, and maintain reliable services.

---

## 4. Key Concepts

### Confidentiality

Confidentiality ensures that sensitive information is accessible only to authorized users.

Common methods used to maintain confidentiality include:

- Authentication
- Authorization
- Encryption
- Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
- Access Control

Example:

Only authorized employees can access company payroll records.

---

### Integrity

Integrity ensures that data remains accurate, complete, and unaltered unless modified by an authorized user.

Common methods include:

- Hashing
- Digital Signatures
- File Permissions
- Version Control

Example:

A banking transaction should not be modified during transmission.

---

### Availability

Availability ensures that systems, applications, and data remain accessible whenever authorized users need them.

Common methods include:

- Regular Backups
- Redundant Systems
- Disaster Recovery Plans
- Load Balancing
- DDoS Protection

Example:

An online banking website should remain available even during periods of high traffic.

---

## 5. Importance of the CIA Triad

The CIA Triad serves as the foundation for information security by helping organizations:

- Protect sensitive data
- Prevent unauthorized modifications
- Ensure continuous access to services
- Reduce cybersecurity risks
- Support security policy development

## 8. Key Takeaways

- The CIA Triad is the foundation of cybersecurity.
- Confidentiality protects sensitive information from unauthorized access.
- Integrity ensures data accuracy and trustworthiness.
- Availability guarantees reliable access to systems and services.
- Effective cybersecurity requires balancing all three principles.
