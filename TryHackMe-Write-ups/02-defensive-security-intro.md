## 2. Objective

The objective of this room is to introduce Defensive Security, explain how organizations protect their systems from cyber threats, and understand the responsibilities of Blue Team professionals.

---

## 3. What is Defensive Security?

Defensive Security focuses on protecting computer systems, networks, and data from cyber attacks. Its primary goal is to prevent, detect, and respond to security incidents before they cause damage.

Unlike Offensive Security, which identifies vulnerabilities by simulating attacks, Defensive Security is responsible for monitoring, securing, and maintaining an organization's IT infrastructure.

---

## 4. Key Concepts

### Blue Team

The Blue Team is responsible for defending an organization's systems against cyber attacks. Their responsibilities include:

- Monitoring systems and networks
- Detecting security threats
- Investigating suspicious activities
- Responding to security incidents

### Security Operations Center (SOC)

A Security Operations Center (SOC) is a dedicated team that continuously monitors an organization's infrastructure for cyber threats and security incidents.

### Threat Detection

Threat detection is the process of identifying malicious activities, unauthorized access, or abnormal behavior before they cause damage.

### Incident Response

Incident Response is the process of identifying, containing, investigating, recovering from, and documenting cybersecurity incidents.

### Digital Forensics

Digital Forensics involves collecting, preserving, analyzing, and presenting digital evidence to understand how an attack occurred.
